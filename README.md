## 👋 About Me

I'm a software engineer and founder of **[Ascension Innovation](https://github.com/DavidJ001)** — a technology company driven by purpose and powered by innovation, based in Nairobi, Kenya. I build custom software, blockchain protocols, and integrated business platforms for clients across East Africa and beyond.

I'm also a certified mental health activist through the [AACC](https://www.aacc.net/about/) — because people matter more than products.

- 📍 Nairobi, Kenya
- 🏗️ Currently building: a decentralised token protocol and an all-in-one business management platform
- 🎨 I draw. 🏊 I swim. 📚 I read — a lot.
